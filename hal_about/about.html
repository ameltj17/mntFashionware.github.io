About Us

M&T Fashionware is one of the leading clothing brands in Indonesia today. This brand itself was originally created by two founders where they had an idea to make a type of clothes that have both fashion aesthetic and comfort.

Why Us?

M&T Fashionware has a mission of its own where we want to make clothes that are not only to look good but feels comfortable when wearing it. Our clothes use 100% cotton to make you get the feeling of premium comfort and softness. Not just that, our clothes are also sustainable.
